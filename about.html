Towards the end of 19th Century in India, to confront the challenges posed by a new era with a view to pursue rejuvenation various movements were initiated. The year 1885 had a unique historical importance. It was during this time that religious, ethical, educational and cultural needs of the society were realized and thus in 1885 Shri Aillak Pannalal Digamber Jain Pathshala was established in Solapur City. Though the institution aimed at imparting religious education, it was backed up by a strong inspiration of promoting national exhortation and national welfare. And hence a broad vision and non-communal motto 'Shikshan Hach Dharma' meaning 'Education is Religion' was sculpted.

The concept of 'Jain Gurukul' inceptionized by Param Pujya Gurudev Samantabhadra Maharaj revolutionized the educational aspect of the Jain community. Educational institutions were duly established chronologically in the following year, 1918- Karanja, 1934- Bahubali, 1940- Solapur.

The Walchand Industrial Group was one of the few conglomerates of the country that brought glory to the pre-independent India by pioneering in industrialization. The founding father of the Walchand Family Late Seth Hirachand Nemchand Doshi also served as the Trustee of the instituion. Today measuring the expansion and excellence of this educational institution,the visionary perception of the pioneers cannot be overlooked.

Gyana, Sheel, Prem Seva and Vyavastha ie Knowledge, Morality, Love, Service and Management are the blissful virtues on which the life of Gurukul is humbly based.

The institution primarily thrives on patriotism, religious devotion and individual well being. Hence Jain Gurukul is not only a boon to the Jain community but it proved to be a pligrimage centre for the entire society

Inspired by the visionary Param Pujya Gurudev the institution of Shri Digamber Jain Gurukul Prashala was established in 1940. In the same premises Boys Hostel for impoverished boys was established. Bearing in mind the social considerations, the technical wing was established in 1961. It was unique in its kind which imparted technical education throughout the city.

Two new high schools in 1961 were started viz. 'Nutan Vidhalaya' at Asti in Solpaur District and' Deshbhushan Kulbhushan Vidyalaya' at Kunthalgiri in Osmanabad District.

The school was basically provisioned for boys but with a genorous view of all round progress of the society, girls were eventually provided with admission.

In the year 1975 a Junior College Wing was established in the same premises. An overall demand from the different strata of the society a pri-primary division was started in 2003 which was gradually extended with primary school in 2004.

VISION

To create knowledgeable, cultured and enlightened citizens through value based and quality education for shaping a progressive society and nation.

MISSION

  • To strive to create bright and promising future for our students through the five Gurukul tenets namely; Knowledge, Conduct, Love, Service and Organization.

  • To inculcate the values of Justice, freedom, equality and fraternity in the students.

  • To make the students competent in vocational education through quality fundamental technical education.

  • To develop a sense of social responsibility, national integrity and global brotherhood among students through co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.

  • To make competent and responsible citizens for fulfilling social and national aspirations.

ACTION

  • To adopt advanced and innovative teaching practices for making learning effective and productive.

  • To strive to develop a sense of self discipline, confidence, respect towards elders and analytical thinking among students.

  • To promote and foster curiosity, scientific attitude and critical thinking in students.

  • To organize various proactive, responsive activities for inculcating morality and developing life skills among students.

  • To develop a sense of cultural heritage in students cherishing fine arts through activities and programmes encouraging a culture of experimentatio.

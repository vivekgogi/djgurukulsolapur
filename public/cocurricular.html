Co-Curricular Activities of the School

CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES OF THE SCHOOL

Sports Day:

The school celebrates the National sports day on the occasion of Major Dhyanchand's birthday, master of hockey. On this occasion various activities like International sports competition, exhibition on the life of great sportperson's and their work as well as lectures are arranged for sports and health consciousness.

Sports Week:

Sports week is celebrated in the month of December on the occasion of Gurukul Din. On this day, school level competitions are arranged like atheletics, football, chess, volleyball etc. Besides, practice of Yoga and Surya Namaskar, physical exercise, practicals, lectures and exhibitions are also conducted

Blood Donation Camp:

The school arranges blood donation camp in the month of December. With the confluence of Gurudev Samant Bhadra Maharaj birth Aninversary and Shriman Bhausaheb Gandhi Pratisthan Solapur.

The parents, teachers and all the students actively participate in this activity. Since last three years consecutively more than 150 people donated the blood. Dr. Hedgewar blood bank helps a lot in this activity.

The school is also honoured for working in blood donation since four years.

National Days:

Following national days are celebrated as Hindi day, National Science day, International Environmental day, Marathi day, National Mathematics day, Engineers day, Earth day, Sanskrit day, wildlife week etc.

Many more activities are arranged on these days. eg. Elocution competition, essay drawing competitions, lectures, question hour, exhibitions, rangoli, Poem singing, handwriting etc. Besides handwritten notes, wall hangings and rallies, are also arranged.

Birth and Death Aninversary:

Various activities are arranged on birth/death aninversary of great people Social activist, National Heroes, Scientists, Mathematician, Sports mans etc.

Teacher's Day:

On the birth ceremony of Dr. Radhakrishnan, Teacher's day is celebrated. All the teachers are felicitated on this day and melava for Ex- teachers is arranged.

Rashtriya Harit Sena:

The school arranged various activities under the above scheme. Wild life week, Environment day, earth day, Ozone day, Environment ralley, essay competition, lectures, exhibition eco-friendly Rang panchmi, eco-friendly Ganesh murthy, creation of Akash kandil, eco-friendly Diwali, seeds collection, drawing exhibition are arranged in school.

CCRT:

The dept of CCRT initiated the activities to make aware the students their culture, to create religious and social toleration, to know about the India's rich culture and heritage.

The CCRT is under Central Ministerys cultural dept. As one of the parts of it, the school has established 'Gurukul Cultural Club' Under this the school arranges exhibitions on India's cultural heritage, India's cultural centres, Indian dance forms, instruments, and folk arts. Besides, the visits are arranged of local artists, their presentation, featured sculptures to identify and know them.

Competitive Exams:

Several competitive exams are arranged and guided by the teacher.

To the students among them NMMS, NTS, IMO, NSO, MTS, Secondary scholarship Exams Navodaya entrance exam, English Marathi, Science, Hindi Sanskrit Exams are also guided and conducted.

The students have achieved grand success continuously in these exams The school has created a great impression in NMMS specially conducted for economically backward students since seven years 44 students are eligible for scholarship.

Gathering and Educational Tour:

The celebration of gathering on Gurukul Day and arrangement of educational tour is notable about the school.

Gathering:

Every year in December cultural programmes are conducted in Hutatma Smurthi Bhavan, special programmes are arranged by music dept. and each class presents an activity among them, group song, group dance, short play, a play, one act play, instrumentation etc. This is a chance for the students to express their art. skill, daring and developments.

Educational Tour:

The school arranged educational tour every year. The school visits historical, religious, geographical, scientific and cultural importance places. The teachers and local guides help the students to understand the significance. The school had visited following places since five years. Ajantha Ellora, Daulatabad, Paithan, Shirdi Bhandardra Kalsubai Peak, Pune, Trimbakeshwar, Astavinayak, Shivneri, Bhimashankar, Pratapgad, Panchgani,Mahabaleshwar, Raigad, Kolhapur, Panhala, Jyotiba, Ratnagiri, Ganpatipule.Murud, Janjara, Mahad etc. The students actually wait for gathering and the tour.

Gurukul Day:

The school celebrate Acharya Samantbhadra Maharaj birth anniversary (19 December) as Gurukul Din. Many more activities are arranged on this auspicious occasion like Blood donation camp, rangoli and drawing competition and gathering, Anand Mela, essay, elocution, hand writing and poem singing competition, question hour, CCRT exhibition etc.
